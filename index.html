The end Of your search is here

Discover Yourself (DYS) is an initiative by Living Meaningful Life (LML), a group of students and alumni of IIT Kharagpur which aims at development of Emotional Quotient (EQ) and Spiritual Quotient (SQ) of an individual. We're guided by our alumni who've reached the zenith of success by following the teachings of Srimad Bhagvat Geeta. We aim to spread Krishna Consiousness and Bhakti not only through sentiments but through scientific evidences and arguments.

Transforming the way you live

DYS comprises of weekly sessions on spirituality and daily sessions of book reading and guided Mahamantra meditations. These sessions aim towards exposing an individual to the spiritual world. This helps in awakening them and make them realise that the ultimate goal of life is NOT just to eat, sleep and be married.

Let's grow together

Spirituality is the key to the utmost bliss. Keeping this in mind, LML always welcomes people who are willing to bring a positive change in their lives. Our mentors themselves have got the benefits of stepping into the fields of spirituality, be it the inner bliss or the intellectual success.
